The Caribbean remains the gold standard for Citizenship by Investment (CBI) programs. In 2026, the landscape has evolved with new pricing regulations and enhanced security protocols, making these passports more valuable than ever.

## Why the Caribbean?

Caribbean nations like St. Kitts & Nevis, Antigua & Barbuda, and Grenada have long been favorites for global investors. They offer:

- **Global Mobility**: Access to over 150 countries visa-free.
- **Tax Efficiency**: Many islands offer zero tax on foreign income, wealth, or inheritance.
- **Fast Processing**: Most programs take between 3 to 6 months to complete.

## Comparing the Programs

### St. Kitts & Nevis
Often called the 'Platinum Brand', St. Kitts recently updated its investment thresholds. It remains a top choice for families looking for a legacy-building asset.

### Grenada
The only Caribbean program with E-2 Treaty status with the USA, allowing citizens to live and work in the United States under certain conditions.
